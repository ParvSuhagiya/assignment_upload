Metro Shoes - About Us

Established in the year of freedom, 1947, Metro Shoes opened up as a footwear store in Colaba, Mumbai and over seven decades, the brand has become a common name in Indian households. Recognised for their skilled craftsmanship, unmatched quality and style quotient, they have the best shoes catering to the diverse tastes of Indian men, women and kids. Metro Shoes is the country’s top footwear brand and is proud of its network of shoe stores in over 100+ cities and an online store providing exclusive fashion footwear and accessories.

Metro is dedicated to designing stylish shoes and accessories that are fashionable and comfortable for customers. Crafted to perfection, Metro’s branded shoes and accessories never fail to amaze. Take a look through our store of online shoes and accessories to bring home a box of joy!

Shoes FAQs

1. What are the most stylish and currently trending shoes online?

For every occasion, stylish designer shoes work as a fashion statement. Vibrant coloured sneakers and modern flats for casuals and glittery ethnic Kolhapuri chappals and heels for parties are trending.

2. What are some tips to buy a shoe online?

Take a good look through the online shoe stores’ collections for options and check their size chart. Consider checking specifications on the sole, heel, material, fastening type and other aspects before buying expensive shoes.

3. Where can I shop for the best shoes online?

Metro’s online shoe stores offer thousands of designs and styles of shoes for various occasions, be it casual wear, parties and festivals, sports, or formal workwear to fulfil your fashion needs.

4. How to measure shoe size?

Place your foot on an A4 paper and make a dot at the heel and longest toe, then measure the distance. Do the same for both feet and check the size chart to find your perfect fit.

5. Can all types of shoes be washed?

Rubber and silicon shoes can be washed while others cannot. However, cleaning and shining products can help you maintain a fresh look easily.

6. Which footwear can be worn in all seasons?

While every season has suitable footwear, ballerinas, sneakers, boots and waterproof sandals are great all-season options.
