Community Guidelines

We are the flagship brand of Sporta Technologies Private Limited, offering Our Services to You wherein You can also participate in various Contests hosted on Our Platform. We, as a Platform, are committed to creating a safe and open environment for expression and giving people a voice on its social communities available on Our Platform.

These guidelines (“Community Guidelines”) set out below provide general guidance and an overview of what is and what is not allowed on Our Platform. Use of the Platform constitutes Your acceptance of these Community Guidelines which may be amended from time to time.

Prohibited Content

The following types of content are prohibited on Our Platform and may warrant action from Us:

Spam

  • Do not post, share, or engage with content at very high frequencies.
  • Do not redirect users to deceptive or harmful websites.
  • Do not link to malware or phishing content.
  • Do not program bots that harm the Platform.
  • Do not repeatedly contact people for commercial purposes without consent.
  • Do not post repetitive comments or “follow me” spam.
  • Do not promote competitors or misleading affiliations.

Misinformation or Scam

  • Use accurate and up-to-date account information.
  • Do not impersonate others or mislead people.
  • Do not post misleading or fake content to deceive users.
  • Do not create fake accounts or maintain multiple accounts.
  • Do not use another person’s details or create fake identities.

Violent and Criminal Behaviour

  • No statements or advocacy of violence.
  • No glorification of hate crimes or terrorism.
  • No misinformation that risks violence or harm.
  • No promotion of bribery, scams, or money laundering.
  • No encouragement of suicide, self-harm, or drug use.

Harassment

  • No bullying, harassment, or threats.
  • No sharing private information like phone numbers or addresses.
  • No derogatory comments or swear words targeting individuals.
  • No mocking of death, disease, or tragedy.

Hate Speech

  • No content that incites hate or violence.
  • No use of slurs or dehumanising speech.
  • No targeting groups based on race, gender, religion, etc.

Copyright Violation

  • Post only original content.
  • Do not copy or reshare others’ teams, data, or screenshots as your own.

Security of Our Platform

  • No hacking or attempts to access others’ accounts.
  • No sharing of personal or confidential information.
  • No distribution of malware or viruses.
  • No phishing or deceptive software.

Offensive Language & Visually Inappropriate Content

  • No sexual or explicit content involving minors.
  • No pornography or nudity.
  • No sexually explicit or derogatory language.

Reporting

If you see any post, comment, or message that violates these Community Guidelines, report it using the Report button on the Platform or contact Help & Support. Repeated violations may lead to account restrictions or bans.

Moderation

Group Admins may remove users violating these guidelines and enable “Only Group Admins can send Message” mode. Dream11 may report risky behaviour to authorities or experts and block access at its discretion.

For more information, please read Our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.
